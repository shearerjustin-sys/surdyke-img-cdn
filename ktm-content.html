Why KTM

What sets KTM apart.

Seventy years of racing, distilled into every bike on the floor.

01
18 Straight Dakars
KTM won the Dakar Rally bike class 18 years running (2001–2019) — a Guinness World Record, and the most dominant streak in rally.
02
500+ World Titles
Seventy years, 500-plus world titles across motocross, supercross, enduro, rally and MotoGP. Racing isn’t marketing here — it’s the business.
03
Fuel-Injected 2-Strokes
KTM built the world’s first mass-production fuel-injected two-stroke off-road bikes — no more pre-mix or jetting, just twist and go.
04
Lighter, Sharper, Faster
A power-to-weight obsession with Brembo brakes and hydraulic clutch standard — the bikes feel as fast as they are.
05
WP Suspension Built In
Every serious KTM rides on WP, the Austrian suspension house inside the KTM group — race-bred damping, dialed from the factory.
06
A Bike for Every Trail
Motocross (SX), cross-country (XC), enduro (EXC), adventure — two- and four-stroke, there’s a Ready to Race machine for your terrain.
